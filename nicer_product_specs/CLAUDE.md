# CLAUDE.md — Shared Context for Nicer Systems Site/Web App

You are working on **Nicer Systems**, an automation + lightweight internal apps agency for admin-heavy American businesses.

## Brand voice
Clear, confident, practical, business-friendly. No hype. Minimal jargon. Translate features into outcomes.

## Tagline
**Primary:** Tell us the problem. We’ll build the system.  
**Backup:** Bring the bottleneck. We’ll automate it.

## The product you are building
A **marketing-optimized landing page** and **web app** that:
- creates a **uniquely different viewing experience** (futuristic, robotic aura, artistic interactions)
- still stays **conversion-first** (clear offer, proof, CTA, lead capture)
- includes **Proof of Work** managed in an **Admin** backend (CMS)
- includes a **visible automation / agentic demo** visitors can interact with
- supports **easy theme customization** in Admin (colors, glow intensity, motion level)
- includes **agent markdown files** in-repo, presented as “our operating system” (optional public view)
- is instrumented for an **end-to-end marketing funnel** (events, UTMs, attribution)

## North-star outcome
Visitors immediately feel:
1) “This is different.”  
2) “They are serious operators.”  
3) “I can see what I’ll get.”  
4) “It’s easy to book and start.”

## Key differentiators to preserve
1. **Brush Reveal Hero** interaction: cursor becomes a brush revealing an underlying layer image.
2. **Workflow Graph ambient motion**: nodes/edges, subtle, robotic.
3. **Visible automation**: interactive “Mini Agent” that turns a bottleneck description into a preview plan.
4. **Proof of work CMS**: easy updates, strong social proof, metrics before/after.
5. **Funnel instrumentation**: track engagement and conversion events end-to-end.

## Constraints (non-negotiable)
- Must be **fast** and accessible (motion has reduced-motion fallbacks).
- Must be **editable** by non-dev in Admin.
- Must be **secure** (Admin auth, content permissions, safe agent outputs).
- Must be **measurable** (analytics events, UTMs, form -> CRM pipeline).

## What “agentic” means here
We are not promising fully autonomous bots with user credentials on day 1.  
We are demonstrating **agent-style system thinking** that generates **draft artifacts**:
- workflow map
- SOP outline
- dashboard KPIs
- automation rules (Zapier/Make)
…and emails the “Preview Plan” to the lead.

## Conversion priorities
Primary CTA: **Book a scoping call**  
Secondary CTA: **Get a Preview Plan** (email capture)

## Out of scope (until Phase 3+)
- Full client portal
- Multi-tenant enterprise RBAC
- Real-time live integrations into a client’s tools without an onboarding step
